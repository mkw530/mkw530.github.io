About Us

Lunazul Surf School & Shop is a family business located on the main beach in Sayulita, Mexico. We give surf lessons, organize surf camps and  have the broadest selection of surfboards and paddleboards for rent in Sayulita and Punta de Mita. We look forward to meeting you and sharing some waves together.
