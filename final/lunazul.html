SURF SCHOOL & SHOP

About Us

Lunazul Surf School & Shop is a family business located on the main beach in Sayulita, Mexico. We give surf lessons, organize surf camps and  have the broadest selection of surfboards and paddleboards for rent in Sayulita and Punta de Mita. We look forward to meeting you and sharing some waves together.

Services

Rentals

Surfboards Galore! Longboards, shortboards, epoxy, foam, fishes, paddle boards and boogie boards. Come visit us and see for yourself!

Lessons

Surfing in Sayulita has never been more fun! Our experienced staff will help you improve, and you'll be confidently playing in the waves in no time. Surf with legendary Lunazul, the first established surf school in Sayulita.

Camps

Lunazul has teamed up with I Love Waves and now offers the best surf camps in Mexico. Video and photo analysis, theory lessons and the best surf instruction you could ask for. We've also thrown in some extra gear and a massage for free!

Trips

A 4hr surf trip to a beautiful beach 20 mins away from Sayulita. We get in our van and go to wherever the waves are good depending on swell, tides and winds.
