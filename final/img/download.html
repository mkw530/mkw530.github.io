Sayulita

Sayulita is a small fishing and surfing village on the west coast of Nayarit, Mexico. The down town plaza is full of brightly coloured mexican restaurants, bars and beautiful people.
With a strong Huichol community, and access to local art, cuisine, and music, Sayulita gives it's visitors the desired experience of traditional Mexican life with a dash of foreign flavor.
